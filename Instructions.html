How to Play

  • Goal: Eat all pellets in the maze while avoiding the ghosts.
  • Controls: Use the arrow keys to move PackMan up, down, left, or right.
  • Power-Up: Eating the larger pellets turns the ghosts blue, making them vulnerable for a short time.
  • Win Condition: Clear all pellets to win the game.
  • Lose Condition: Lose a life if a ghost catches you.
